Lasagna

Description

Layers of meaty sauce, ricotta cheese, parmesan, and lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 28oz can tomato sauce
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • 1 14 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp chopped fresh oregano OR
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, packed
  • 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder AND/OR
  • 1 pinch garlic salt
  • 2 Tbsp red OR white wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 lb (9 pcs) dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 lb (24 oz) mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • 1/4 lb (4 oz) freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Put a large pot of salted water on the stovetop on high heat. Prepare the sauce while the water is heating up.

  2. In a large skillet, heat 2 Tbsp of olive oil on medium high heat. Add the ground beef and cook until lightly browned on all sides. Remove beef from heat with a slotted spoon to a bowl. Retain 1 Tbsp of fat and discard the rest.

  3. Add the bell pepper and onions. Cook for 4 to 5 minutes, until onions are translucent and the peppers are softened. Add the garlic and cook another half a minute.

  4. Return the beef to the pan and combine. Reduce heat and cook 5 minutes.

  5. Transfer the beef to a pot. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Add parsley, oregano, and italian seasoning. Sprinkle with garlic powder and/or garlic salt.Salt to taste but note Parmesan will later add saltiness. Sprinkle with vinegar. Add sugar 1 Tbsp at a time, to taste.

  6. Bring the sauce to simmer, lower heat and cook from 15 to 45 minutes stirring often and making sure the bottom doesn't scorch. Remove from heat.

  7. Cook noodles to al dente.

  8. Drain and seperate noodles, place on rack or use a little olive oil on parchment to prevent sticking while cooling.

  9. Heat oven to 375 F

  10. In a 9x13 baking dish, spread a cup of sauce across the bottom. Arrange one layer of lasagna noodles lengthwise. Add 1/3 of the sauce on top of the noodles.

  11. Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella over the sauce. Add half of the ricotta in dollops about an inch ore so apart.

  12. Add another layer of noodles and repeat the last step.

  13. Top with alayer of mozzarella.

  14. Cover the pan with aluminum foil tented so it doesn't touch lasagna and bake for 45 minutes. Uncover for last 10 minutes to crisp top.
